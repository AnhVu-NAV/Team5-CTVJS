About Our Website

Are you tired of struggling alone or struggling to fit your classes into your own schedule.
We get it. College can be tough,especially when you're stuck in a class with no one to
share the load. That's where we come in!

Our website helps you connect with other students who taking the classes you want.
With just three easy steps:

1. Find by Subject: Simply enter the subject name and we'll search through our database
to list all options of that subject.

2. Find an option: Base on the list, look for an option that meets your needs.

3. Connect or create: If you find a suitable option, connect with them directly through our platform .No Match? No worries! Create a new request .
